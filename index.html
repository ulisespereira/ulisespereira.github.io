About me

I am postdoctoral associate at the Xiao-Jing Wang's Lab at the Center of Neural Since at New York University. Previously, I was a PhD student at the Department of Statistics at the University of Chicago, advised by Nicolas Brunel. I am Latinamerican, originally from Chile.

You will find more about my academic background on my CV.
