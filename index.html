About me

I am a Swartz Fellow in Theoretical Neuroscience at the Center of Neural Since at New York University. I build mechanistic network models for cognitive processes in close collaboration with experimentalists. I use tools from applied mathematics, nonlinear dynamics and statistical physics to analyze these models and provide testable experimental predictions.
